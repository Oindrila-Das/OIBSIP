Aditya Mitra

1953 - 2018

Guru Aditya Mitra

The Bharatnatyam Dancer of India

Late Guru Aditya Mitra a very passionate Bharatnatyam dancer was born on 5th January 1953 . He passed away on 8th June 2018. He passed away at an early age of 65 because he was going through dialysis and couldn't take it. He was not only a dance guru but also an inspirational person for people to admire him. With the help of him a lot of people came out as incredible dancers. He established his school on the year 1980 with all his hardwork and dedication. He named his school as Indian Cultural Ensemble. The school was not only based on Bharatnatyam but also Kathak, Rabindra Nritya. It also had drawing classes and singing classes. He also published his own book Prashnottare Bharatnatyam which describes all the mudras of dance and different postures of Bharatnatyam, history of Bharatnatyam. He also explained different dance expressions.
